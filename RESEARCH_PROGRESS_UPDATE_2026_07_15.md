## Research Challenges Encountered & Solutions

### Challenge 1: Route Name Aliases
**Problem:** Routes known by multiple names (local, guidebook, Mountain Project)
**Solution:** Normalize names and cross-reference by GPS coordinates

### Challenge 2: Seasonal Variability
**Problem:** Ice conditions vary dramatically year-to-year
**Solution:** Use NWAC seasonal patterns, multi-year trip reports, historical incident data

### Challenge 3: Sparse Remote Documentation
**Problem:** Backcountry/lesser-known routes have few sources
**Solution:** Prioritize high-traffic areas first; use guide service documentation for remote routes

### Challenge 4: Access Changes
**Problem:** Closures and permits change seasonally
**Solution:** Query NWAC, NPS, local rangers for current access; note seasonal closures in watch_out
