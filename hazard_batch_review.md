## ⚠️ Read this first — attribution cautions

Several AAC-documented accidents were initially associated with a route but actually occurred on an **adjacent** route on the same peak. These should NOT be written to the named route unless noted otherwise:
- Mount Adams: the AAC crevasse-fall accident is on the **Mazama Glacier**, not South Spur.
- Glacier Peak: one AAC crevasse accident is on the **Vista Glacier**, not Sitkum.
- Mount Stuart: the 2020 rockfall/rescue accident is on the **North Ridge**, not West Ridge.

## Cluster 4 — Crag/area-level (from the earlier scoping agent, larger blast radius)

Covers **~150-300 WA sport/trad/boulder crags**, starting with 8 already researched (Index Town Wall, Frenchman Coulee, Peshastin Pinnacles, Exit 38/Deception Crags, Mount Erie, Tieton/Royal Columns, Beacon Rock, Leavenworth/Icicle Canyon). This would write to potentially 50+ routes per crag area, not single routes — separate approval since the scope multiplier is much larger. Highlights: a 2010 corroded-bolt failure at Index ("Calling Wolfgang"), a 2003 fatal accident at Frenchman Coulee (Goran Kropp, gear failure), a 2017 rockfall closure at Tieton/Royal Columns, and 3 documented fatalities at Beacon Rock/Snow Creek Wall combined.
